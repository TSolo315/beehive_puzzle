Beehive Puzzle Instructions

  • The goal is to repopulate the hive in as few turns as possible and by placing as few bees as possible.
  • You can place a bee on the board by clicking on a hexagonal tile.
  • A tile that is surrounded by three bee tiles will be converted into a bee tile.
  • When you click simulate tiles will be converted in turns until either no tiles are converted or the entire hive has been repopulated.
